About Me

Art was a passion...it still is. I graduated from Bowie State University with a Fine/Visual arts degree. Fast-forward several years later and I'm embarking on a new journey to become a Full Stack Web Developer. Both admittedly and unfortunately, I was one of those people that never knew what they wanted to be, or what their purpose was. It's of course never too late. So, after many years of researching and trying different fields that I, in turn, determined I had little interest in or didn't suit my personality, my father came across information for a coding Bootcamp. I thought back to (and yes, this next statement ages me a bit) MySpace days. Opening the code template and messing about in it. Trial and error, changing things, making text blink, or scroll, embed a music player, etc. and it came to me! Combine the back end with the front end. Coding structure with front end design aided by my past art passion(degree). Combining the visual aesthetic with the back end functionality. Well to that I say, here goes something!
